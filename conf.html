Confession of Love

I've kept it hidden, deep within my heart,
This feeling I have, I just can't depart.
Every time I see you, my heart skips a beat,
And I know the time has come for me to speak.

I know it's scary, but I must be brave,
To reveal my feelings, I must find a way.
So here I am, standing before you,
With a heart overflowing, and a love so true.

I hope you see, the passion I hold,
And understand the story that must be told.
That you are special, and mean the world to me,
And with you by my side, my heart will always be free.

So here is my confession, I hope you see,
That I've fallen in love, and it's all because of thee.
And now I wait, with bated breath,
For your reply, and what lies ahead.

This is a moment, I'll never forget,
And I hope that our love will never regret.
So I'll leave it in your hands, and pray you'll understand,
That confessing my love, was the best thing I ever planned.
